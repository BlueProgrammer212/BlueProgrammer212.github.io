What is Pixcel?

Pixcel is a free pixel art application for talented pixel artists and game developers. We would love to hear from your suggestions and feedback!

How to get started?

Download the latest offline version in the download page and click the latest version, then have fun drawing. Or you can always use the online version in our online pixcel editor. Before using our application, you must agree to our terms and conditions. If you need help or clarrifications, go to https://blueprogrammer212.github.io/home#help . If something went wrong address the issue in https://blueprogrammer212.github.io/home/comments/
